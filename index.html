ABOUT US

Founded in 2012, We are a kick-ass dedicated team of developers, designers, architects, and most importantly gaming enthusiasts. We've been playing the hottest video games on the market for the last 20+ years, we found out it was pretty sweet to work on them too.

We cut our teeth in the educational gaming space, and now we've done work on big screens, small screens, and every screen in between. We're based in Schenectady, NY - part of the Capital Region's game development hub and we're just getting warmed up.

OUR SERVICES

We collaborate with game developers and publishers and have experiencing working on AAA game clients (web based / console integration), game-data disaster recovery solutions, community based tools, and more. Our developers have a deep understanding of what it means to be a player, and that makes a big difference in how we approach and understand different client needs.


Whether you’re looking to scale your existing development team or you want to outsource an entire project, JG is the company for you. We have a proven track record of completing projects on time, within budget, and without annoying our clients... which is pretty unique if you know anything about the software industry.
